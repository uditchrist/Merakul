    About the Company

    Why we?

    In today's era, families are becoming single due to which most of us are forgetting the joint family and its utilities.
    Merakul.com aims to bridge this distance between families and reconnect them with culture and customs through various activities.

    • Connecting Family Generations
    • Lack of knowledge of family background
    • Collecting memories permanently
    • Recording family customs
    • Remembering important milestones and dates

    It is often seen that it is difficult for humans to remember the fourth fifth generation. It is a far cry to remember the activities and efforts of the fourth and fifth generation, due to which the family heritage is getting extinct in various forms. Merakul.com's effort is to stop this extinction in the knowledge of generations and their heritage and make it accessible to the next generation in future.
